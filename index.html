Welcome to The BeautifulStore Foundation


Beautiful Store provides jobs to people with disabilities using the items you donate. At 22 Beautiful Store branches, 333 disabled people received donations, donation center, collection of donated items, logistics of donated items. I am working happily and happily in various departments such as merchandising and sales.

“Everyone is having a hard time right now! Without the Beautiful Store, where would I be and what would I be doing?

I am happy to be able to come out and work with my colleagues, and I hope to work with more friends.”

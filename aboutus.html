We are reaching out for a better world



Beautiful Store



We have big dreams. In the future, Beautiful Store will continue to expand its scope and create jobs for about 3,000 people with developmental disabilities through 100 stores nationwide, and will allow disabled people and their families to equally enjoy the 'daily life' that ordinary people enjoy. Furthermore, we have a vision to create a community village where disabled and non-disabled people live together and practice service and sharing until the end of their lives. Beautiful Store will do its best to fulfill this precious mission. We ask for your continued interest and love in the future.



VISION

Win-Win

In order to practice the spirit of Christianity, we provide jobs to people with disabilities. Helps you achieve spiritual stability, restoration of self-esteem, and financial independence.

MISSION

Creation of positions for the disabled

‘Opportunity, not charity,’ Beautiful Store, which started with the slogan ‘Opportunity, not charity,’ began with the good purpose of creating jobs where the developmentally disabled and the socially disadvantaged could become self-sufficient.

Mind Camp

Strengthening independence and independence

Through the Hanmaeum Camp once a year, we provide disabled employees with the experience of communicating with the world, strengthening their independence, and collaborating with each other. Although we were unable to proceed in 2020 due to the coronavirus, we hope that the coronavirus will disappear quickly and the camp will proceed. “When my child first started working, I had to be by her side at all times. “Now there are so many things I can do on my own, so I’m so happy and proud.”

Environmental Protection

Donating unused items will greatly help protect the environment.

We have lost a lot due to environmental changes such as the increase in fine dust and water pollution, and we are facing even greater environmental changes. Fuel and various chemicals are used to make items. And even when the product has reached the end of its useful life and has to be disposed of, it causes great pollution. If we use our daily necessities sparingly, we will make fewer of them. Practices like these will prevent unnecessary waste of resources and help prevent environmental pollution.
